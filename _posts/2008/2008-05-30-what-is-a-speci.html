Wilkins mt_id: 3846 blog_id: 2 post_id: 3846 basename: what_is_a_speci --- If somebody asked me to write a short essay giving an overview of my favourite topic, the nature of species, I doubt that I could. He has an essay on species in the current edition of Scientific American. And despite quoting some obscure Australian philosopher, it is a good summary of the issues.
